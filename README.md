# bussiness_dashboard
Design a Business Dashboard and Fraud Detection

Insights

Total revenue provides a clear view of overall business performance.
Sales show monthly variations, indicating seasonal demand.
A few products generate most of the revenue.
Repeat customers contribute significantly to sales.
Revenue is concentrated in a few countries, highlighting key target markets.

Recommendations

Maintain inventory for high-demand products.
Strengthen marketing in top-performing regions.
Offer loyalty programs to retain valuable customers.
Use seasonal sales trends for better inventory and promotion planning.
